good to know

a few things to make the weekend smoother

🌡️ Early-June Weather

  • Hot and very humid: highs around 89–91°F, lows around 74°F
  • Short, heavy afternoon thunderstorms are common
  • Long days: sun up around 5:58 AM, down around 8:00 PM

🧳 Pack For It

  • Light breathable outfits and real walking shoes
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses, refillable water bottle
  • Compact umbrella or poncho
  • Swimsuit for the private pool
  • Cash for performers, tips, vendors, and queens

👗 Dress Codes

  • Sat night: Divas — pick your favorite decade
  • Sun brunch: Drag — show up and show out
  • Pres Hall: casual and comfy; it can get warm inside

🚕 Travel Time Cheat Sheet

  • Home Base / nearby breakfast → MSY: 25–40 min by car
  • Home Base → French Quarter: 8–15 min by car, 25–35 min walking
  • Home Base → Frenchmen St: 10–15 min by car
  • Home Base → The Country Club: 10–18 min by car
  • Loretta’s locations: Rampart is Saturday; French Market is Sunday

💧 Stay Human

  • Alternate water between drinks
  • Pace edibles in the heat
  • Snack early so brunch cocktails do not sneak up

⚜️ NOLA Etiquette

  • Tip your performers: drag queens and jazz musicians run on cash
  • To-go cups are legal; open glass containers on the street are not
  • Lock dinner reservations as soon as possible
